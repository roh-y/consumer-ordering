## Choosing the Right Plan

### Choose Basic if you:
- Primarily use Wi-Fi and need minimal mobile data
- Want the lowest monthly cost
- Use your phone mainly for calls, texts, and light browsing
- Have a secondary phone line
- Typically use less than 5 GB of data per month

### Choose Standard if you:
- Use social media, stream music, and browse the web regularly
- Want 5G access at an affordable price
- Need moderate hotspot capability for occasional laptop use
- Are an average smartphone user
- Typically use 10-25 GB of data per month

### Choose Premium if you:
- Stream video frequently on your phone
- Work remotely and depend on mobile data
- Want priority network access to avoid congestion
- Travel domestically and want generous hotspot data
- Typically use 25-75 GB of data per month

### Choose Unlimited if you:
- Never want to worry about data limits
- Stream 4K video, play online games, or create content
- Travel internationally frequently
- Want all perks included (streaming, international calling, trade-in credit)
- Use your phone as a primary internet source
- Typically use 75+ GB of data per month

## Upgrade Path

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time with no fees:
- **Basic → Standard**: Gain 5G access and 5x more data for $20/month more
- **Standard → Premium**: Get priority data, 3x more data, and streaming perks for $20/month more
- **Premium → Unlimited**: Remove all data caps and get maximum perks for $20/month more

## Cost Savings Tips

1. **Use Wi-Fi when available** — Connect to trusted Wi-Fi networks to conserve your mobile data allowance
2. **Monitor your usage** — Check your data consumption in your device settings to choose the right plan
3. **Start with Standard** — Our most popular plan offers the best balance of features and cost for most users
4. **Family considerations** — Each line requires its own plan. For families, consider mixing plans based on each member's usage
