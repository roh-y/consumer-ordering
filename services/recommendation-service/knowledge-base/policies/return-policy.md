# Return & Cancellation Policy

## 14-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your wireless service. If for any reason you're not happy, you can cancel within 14 days of your initial plan activation for a full refund.

### How to Use the Guarantee
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to the "Orders" page
3. Click "Cancel Order" on your active order
4. If within 14 days of activation, a full refund will be processed

### Refund Processing
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- The refund is returned to the original payment method
- You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is initiated

## Cancellation After 14 Days

### How It Works
- You can cancel your plan at any time from the "Orders" page
- No early termination fees or cancellation penalties
- Service remains active until the end of your current billing period
- No refund for the current billing cycle after the 14-day guarantee period

### What Happens to Your Account
- Your user account remains active — you can log in anytime
- Your profile information is preserved
- You can resubscribe to any plan at any time by placing a new order
- Order history is retained for your records

## Plan Changes vs. Cancellation

### Changing Plans
If you're not satisfied with your current plan but want to continue service, consider changing plans instead of cancelling:
- No fee to change plans
- Changes can be made anytime from the "Orders" page
- Pricing is prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle

### Downgrading
If cost is a concern, downgrading to a lower-tier plan may be a better option than cancelling entirely. All plans include unlimited calls and texts.

## Device Financing

### Cancellation with Active Device Financing
If you cancel your plan while under a device financing agreement (Premium and Unlimited plans):
- Remaining device payments continue as scheduled
- Device financing is a separate agreement from your wireless plan
- Contact support for options regarding device payment

## Contact Us

For questions about cancellations, refunds, or our return policy, use the chat widget on our website or contact customer support during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST).
