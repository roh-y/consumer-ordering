# Basic Plan

## Overview
The Basic Plan is our most affordable wireless option, designed for light users who primarily need voice calling and texting with minimal data usage.

## Pricing
- **Monthly Cost**: $29.99/month
- **No contract required** — cancel anytime
- **No activation fee**

## Data Allowance
- **5 GB** of high-speed 4G LTE data per month
- After 5 GB, speeds reduced to 2G (128 Kbps) for remainder of billing cycle
- No data rollover to next month

## Features
- Unlimited domestic calls and texts
- Voicemail included
- Wi-Fi calling supported
- 100 MB international roaming data (select countries)
- Mobile hotspot: 1 GB included

## Best For
- Light smartphone users
- Users who primarily use Wi-Fi at home and work
- Budget-conscious customers
- Secondary/backup phone lines
- Seniors or young users with minimal data needs

## Limitations
- No 5G access
- Limited mobile hotspot (1 GB)
- No international calling included (available as add-on at $10/month)
- No device financing available
- No premium streaming perks
- Throttled speeds after data cap

## Network
- 4G LTE coverage nationwide
- No priority data — may experience slower speeds during network congestion

## How to Subscribe
Customers can sign up for the Basic Plan through our website by browsing the Plans page, selecting the Basic Plan, and completing the order process. An active account is required.
