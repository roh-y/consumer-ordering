# Standard Plan

## Overview
The Standard Plan is our most popular option, offering a balanced mix of data, speed, and features for everyday smartphone users.

## Pricing
- **Monthly Cost**: $49.99/month
- **No contract required** — cancel anytime
- **No activation fee**

## Data Allowance
- **25 GB** of high-speed 5G/4G LTE data per month
- After 25 GB, speeds reduced to 3G (1 Mbps) for remainder of billing cycle
- No data rollover to next month

## Features
- Unlimited domestic calls and texts
- Voicemail and visual voicemail included
- Wi-Fi calling supported
- 5G network access included
- 5 GB mobile hotspot data
- 500 MB international roaming data (50+ countries)
- Free caller ID and spam blocking
- HD voice calling

## Best For
- Everyday smartphone users
- Users who stream music and use social media regularly
- Remote workers who need reliable mobile connectivity
- Families looking for a mid-tier option
- Users who want 5G access without paying premium prices

## Limitations
- Data speeds throttled after 25 GB cap
- No premium streaming subscriptions included
- International calling not included (available as add-on at $10/month)
- Device financing available only for select models
- Not ideal for heavy video streaming or gaming on mobile data

## Network
- 5G and 4G LTE coverage nationwide
- Standard priority data — may experience deprioritization during peak congestion

## How to Subscribe
Customers can sign up for the Standard Plan through our website by browsing the Plans page, selecting the Standard Plan, and completing the order process. An active account is required.
