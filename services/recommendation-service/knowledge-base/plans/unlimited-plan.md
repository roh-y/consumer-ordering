# Unlimited Plan

## Overview
The Unlimited Plan is our top-tier offering with truly unlimited high-speed data, maximum perks, and the best network priority for users who demand the most from their wireless service.

## Pricing
- **Monthly Cost**: $89.99/month
- **No contract required** — cancel anytime
- **No activation fee**

## Data Allowance
- **Unlimited** high-speed 5G/4G LTE data — no throttling, no caps
- Truly unlimited — no speed reductions at any usage level
- Network management may apply in rare extreme congestion scenarios

## Features
- Unlimited domestic calls and texts
- Voicemail and visual voicemail included
- Wi-Fi calling supported
- 5G network access with highest priority data
- 50 GB mobile hotspot data
- 5 GB international roaming data (100+ countries)
- International calling to 30+ countries included
- Free caller ID and spam blocking
- HD voice calling and Wi-Fi calling
- Premium streaming bundle (music + video streaming included)
- Device financing available for all models — 0% APR
- Priority customer support with dedicated line
- Annual device trade-in credit ($200 value)
- Free smartwatch data line

## Best For
- Heavy data users with no desire to monitor usage
- Gamers and video streamers
- Professionals who need reliable, fast connectivity everywhere
- Frequent international travelers
- Families who want the best plan with maximum hotspot sharing
- Content creators and social media professionals
- Users who want all perks included without add-ons

## Limitations
- Highest monthly cost of all plans
- Hotspot still capped at 50 GB (then reduced to 3 Mbps)
- International roaming beyond 5 GB charged at $10/GB
- Some streaming perks require separate app downloads

## Network
- 5G and 4G LTE coverage nationwide
- Highest priority data — first in line during network congestion
- Optimized for 4K video streaming and low-latency gaming
- Access to mmWave 5G in supported areas for ultra-fast speeds

## How to Subscribe
Customers can sign up for the Unlimited Plan through our website by browsing the Plans page, selecting the Unlimited Plan, and completing the order process. An active account is required.
