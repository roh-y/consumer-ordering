# Premium Plan

## Overview
The Premium Plan delivers a high-end wireless experience with generous data, priority network access, and bundled entertainment perks for power users.

## Pricing
- **Monthly Cost**: $69.99/month
- **No contract required** — cancel anytime
- **No activation fee**

## Data Allowance
- **75 GB** of premium high-speed 5G/4G LTE data per month
- After 75 GB, speeds reduced to 5 Mbps (still usable for streaming) for remainder of billing cycle
- No data rollover to next month

## Features
- Unlimited domestic calls and texts
- Voicemail and visual voicemail included
- Wi-Fi calling supported
- 5G network access with priority data
- 25 GB mobile hotspot data
- 2 GB international roaming data (75+ countries)
- Free caller ID and spam blocking
- HD voice calling
- Free basic streaming subscription (music streaming included)
- Device financing available for all models
- Priority customer support line

## Best For
- Power users and heavy data consumers
- Video streamers (Netflix, YouTube, TikTok)
- Remote workers who rely heavily on mobile data
- Frequent domestic travelers
- Users who want premium features without unlimited pricing
- Small business owners

## Limitations
- Not truly unlimited — speeds reduced after 75 GB
- International calling not included (available as add-on at $5/month — discounted)
- Premium streaming (video) not included
- Hotspot limited to 25 GB

## Network
- 5G and 4G LTE coverage nationwide
- Priority data access — less likely to experience congestion slowdowns
- Optimized for HD video streaming on mobile

## How to Subscribe
Customers can sign up for the Premium Plan through our website by browsing the Plans page, selecting the Premium Plan, and completing the order process. An active account is required.
