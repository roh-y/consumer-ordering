# Billing FAQ

## Payment & Billing

### When am I billed?
You are billed monthly on the anniversary of your subscription start date. For example, if you subscribed on March 15, your billing date each month is the 15th.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and debit cards. Payment information is securely processed through our payment provider.

### How do I view my billing history?
Log in to your account and navigate to the "Orders" page. You can view all past and current orders, including their status and pricing details.

### Can I get a refund?
Refunds are available within 14 days of initial plan activation if you are not satisfied with your service. After 14 days, you can cancel your plan at any time, but previously billed amounts are non-refundable. See our Return & Cancellation Policy for full details.

## Plan Changes & Pricing

### How much does it cost to change plans?
There is no fee to change plans. Simply go to the Orders page and select "Change Plan." The price difference is prorated for the remainder of your current billing cycle.

### If I upgrade mid-cycle, do I pay the full new price?
No. When you upgrade mid-cycle, you pay a prorated amount for the remaining days in your current billing cycle. Starting the next full billing cycle, you'll be charged the new plan's full monthly price.

### If I downgrade, do I get a credit?
Yes. When you downgrade mid-cycle, the price difference for remaining days is credited to your next bill.

### Are there any hidden fees?
No. The monthly price listed for each plan is the complete cost. There are no activation fees, no administrative fees, and no early termination fees. Taxes may apply based on your location.

## Add-Ons

### What add-ons are available?
- **International Calling**: $10/month (or $5/month on Premium plan) — unlimited calls to 30+ countries
- **Extra Hotspot Data**: $10 for an additional 10 GB hotspot block
- **International Roaming Extra**: $10/GB beyond your plan's included roaming data

### How do I add or remove add-ons?
Contact our support team through the chat widget or manage add-ons from your Profile page (coming soon).

## Cancellation

### How do I cancel my plan?
Go to the "Orders" page, find your active order, and click "Cancel Order." Your service will remain active through the end of your current billing period.

### Will I be charged after cancellation?
No. Once cancelled, you will not be charged for subsequent billing cycles. Your service remains active until the end of the current paid period.

### Can I reactivate a cancelled plan?
Yes. Simply place a new order for any plan. Your account and profile information are preserved even after cancellation.
