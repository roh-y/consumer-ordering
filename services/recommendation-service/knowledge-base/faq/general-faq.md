# General FAQ

## Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?
Visit our website and click "Register." You'll need to provide your email address, first name, last name, and create a password. A confirmation code will be sent to your email — enter it to verify your account.

### How do I log in?
Click "Login" on the website and enter your registered email address and password. After logging in, you'll have access to your profile, plans, and orders.

### I forgot my password. What do I do?
Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox to reset your password.

### How do I update my profile information?
After logging in, navigate to the "Profile" page. You can update your first name, last name, phone number, and address.

## Plans

### What plans do you offer?
We offer four wireless plans:
1. **Basic** ($29.99/month) — 5 GB data, 4G LTE, best for light users
2. **Standard** ($49.99/month) — 25 GB data, 5G access, best for everyday users
3. **Premium** ($69.99/month) — 75 GB data, priority 5G, best for power users
4. **Unlimited** ($89.99/month) — Unlimited data, highest priority, best for heavy users

### Can I change my plan after subscribing?
Yes! You can change your plan at any time from the "Orders" page by selecting "Change Plan." The new plan takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

### Is there a contract or commitment?
No. All our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime without early termination fees.

### Do unused data roll over to the next month?
No. Data allowances reset at the beginning of each billing cycle. Unused data does not carry over.

## Orders

### How do I place an order?
Browse our Plans page, select the plan you want, and click "Subscribe." You'll be guided through the order process. You must be logged in to place an order.

### How do I check my order status?
Go to the "Orders" page after logging in. You'll see all your orders with their current status (Pending, Active, Cancelled, etc.).

### Can I cancel an order?
Yes. From the "Orders" page, select the order you want to cancel and click "Cancel Order." Note that cancellation is only available for orders that haven't been fully activated.

### How long does order activation take?
Most orders are activated within minutes. Some orders may take up to 24 hours during peak periods.

## Network & Coverage

### What network technology do you use?
We operate on 4G LTE and 5G networks nationwide. 5G access is available on Standard, Premium, and Unlimited plans.

### What happens when I exceed my data limit?
Your data speed is reduced for the remainder of the billing cycle. The reduced speed depends on your plan — Basic slows to 128 Kbps, Standard to 1 Mbps, and Premium to 5 Mbps. The Unlimited plan has no data cap.

## Support

### How do I contact customer support?
You can use the chat widget on our website to get instant help from our AI assistant. The assistant can answer questions about plans, check your order status, and help you find the right plan.

### What are your support hours?
Our AI chat assistant is available 24/7. For complex issues requiring human support, our team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST.
